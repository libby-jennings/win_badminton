Squad Training Expectations

  • Attend both training sessions(inform your coach if ill and unable to attend as a one off)
  • Arrive 5-10 minutes early to your sessions
  • Wear your performance centre kit
  • Always have two spare rackets in your bag, spare kit and a large drinks bottle
  • Always bring a healthy carbohydrate/protein snack to have during or immediately after training (banana, cereal bar, healthy milkshake, small sandwich etc).
  • Show respect to staff, coaches and other players at the badminton centre (take outdoor shoes off and wait until play is finished before walking behind courts)
  • Maintain a consistent effort to become the best player you can be

    • Academy Access Sessions

    These sessions are for developing and emerging players who want to supplement their squad sessions with additional training either on-court or in the gym with individualised coaching.
